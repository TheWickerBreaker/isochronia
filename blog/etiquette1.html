The Art Of Giving A Pleasant Dinner Party...

"A good dinner sharpens the wit... while it softens the heart"

-Doran

Precedence is given to dinner parties in the ranks of entertainment's... inasmuch as more dinners are given, most people enjoy them... and they are of far greater social weight than any other method of recieving friends and neighbourss...

Husbands and wives... we know... are not always like loving birds... who in their nests agree... but in the matter of dinner-giving they must agree... or woe betide the luckless guests...
